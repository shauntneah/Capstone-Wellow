Project Overview

Project Wellow is a responsive, Minimum Viable Product (MVP) web application that aims to empower a fresh produce wholesaler to make B2B transactions while also providing an online presence to better brand the business.
This capstone was collaboratively built and completed by a team of three, as part of a GenSG Junior Full Stack Developer Bootcamp (6th cohort).
This capstone project was built collaboratively with applied learning using: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, Java, Spring Boot, and SQL.

'Wellow' Business Context

'Wellow' is a pseudo, haute-couture produce wholesaler based in Singapore.
The business prides itself on providing the highest quality, unique, and exclusive produce to its discerning clientele in South East Asia. Clients include artisan chefs, star-chefs, omakases, fine-dining restaurants, and top hotels.
The business specialise in sourcing and providing a wide variety of premium ingredients that will elevate the taste, presentation, and overall dining experience of our clients' dishes. To ensure optimal freshness and quality, we work directly with farmers and growers to actively source the freshest and most unique produce to offer our clients. It also operate & grow a select range of culinary ingridents in our in-house, clean-room smart vertical farming facility.
